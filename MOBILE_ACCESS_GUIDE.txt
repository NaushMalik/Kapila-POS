# KAPILA INVOICE - MOBILE ACCESS GUIDE

## EASIEST WAY - Just 2 Steps:

### Step 1: Open a new terminal/command prompt
Go to your KapilaInvoice folder and run:
```
python app.py
```
Keep this window open!

### Step 2: Open ANOTHER new terminal/command prompt
Go to your KapilaInvoice folder and run:
```
npx localtunnel --port 5001
```

### Step 3: Use the URL!
- You will see a URL like: `https://something.loca.lt`
- Open that URL on your mobile phone
- That's it! You're done!

## LOGIN CREDENTIALS:

## NOTE:
- Every time you restart, you get a NEW URL
- Keep your laptop on and connected to internet
- The URL works on 4G/5G too - not just your WiFi!

## ALTERNATIVE - Using ngrok (permanent URL):
1. Sign up at https://ngrok.com
2. Get your auth token
3. Run: `ngrok authtoken YOUR_TOKEN`
4. Run: `ngrok http 5001`
5. You'll get a permanent URL!
